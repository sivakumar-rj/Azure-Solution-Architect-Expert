# How to Find Working Video Tutorials

## ⚠️ Important Note About Video Links

Video links on YouTube frequently change or get deleted. Instead of providing links that may break, here's how to **always find the latest, working videos**:

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1: Go to YouTube
Open: **youtube.com**

### Step 2: Search for Content

**For Complete Courses:**
```
Search: "AZ-305 complete course 2026"
Search: "Azure Solutions Architect Expert full course"
Search: "John Savill AZ-305"
```

**For Specific Topics:**
```
Search: "Azure AKS tutorial 2026"
Search: "Azure Firewall complete guide"
Search: "Cosmos DB tutorial"
```

### Step 3: Filter Results
- Click **Filters**
- Select **Upload date** → **This year** or **This month**
- Sort by **View count** (most popular first)

### Step 4: Check Video Quality
Look for videos with:
- ✅ High view count (100K+ views)
- ✅ Good like ratio
- ✅ Recent upload date (2025-2026)
- ✅ Long duration (30+ minutes for tutorials)
- ✅ Verified channels

✅ **Filter by duration:** 20+ minutes for deep dives
